WHAT?

A senior club-inspired non-profit organizing sightseeing trips and excursions around Amsterdam and Netherlands.

WHY?

We want to help people get out of their routines, meet new people, and discover new places.

FOR WHO?

Although SiA's meant to be a community for exapats/foreigners/immigrants/long-time graduates (however you identify) - Dutch people are also more than welcome!

WHERE?

The starting point of most excursions will be in central Amsterdam. Each invitation will specify a destination, and whether we go there by bike, train or both.

WHEN?

Our goal is to organize at least one excursion/sightseeing/meet-up per month.

HOW?

Simply subscribe to our newsletter and you will receive monthly invites in your mail. Each invite will contain the following info: 🚲 TITLE 🎟 Activity 🗓 Date 🕙 Time 📍 Meeting Point 💸 Est. Cost 👥 Max number of attendees 💬 RSVP to let us know if you're coming If you're a SiA club member, you will be given priority in case of limited attendance.
